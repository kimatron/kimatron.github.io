About Me

From the depths of the ocean to the cloud, my journey has been anything but conventional. With a background in marine conservation and over a decade of experience as a scuba instructor and technical diver, I've always been driven by curiosity and a desire to make a positive impact.

Now, I'm channeling that same passion into the world of software development. As a full-stack developer with expertise in Microsoft Azure cloud computing, I bring a unique perspective to every project. My experience in high-pressure underwater environments has honed my problem-solving skills and attention to detail - qualities that translate perfectly into writing clean, efficient code and designing robust cloud solutions.

I'm excited to merge my love for technology with my commitment to conservation, creating innovative solutions that can make a difference in both the digital and natural worlds.
